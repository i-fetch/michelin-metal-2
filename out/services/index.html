WHAT WE DO

OUR SERVICES

End-to-end metal recycling solutions — from collection and sorting to baling, logistics and global bulk distribution.

FULL-SPECTRUM CAPABILITY

EVERYTHING METAL, END-TO-END

Metal Sourcing & Collection

We operate an extensive sourcing network across West Africa to collect and acquire scrap metal from auto wreckers, demolition sites, factories, and households.

  • On-site collection and pickup
  • Bulk acquisition at competitive rates
  • Sourcing from vehicles, appliances & construction
  • Wide network across Anambra, Lagos, and South-East Nigeria
  • Transparent pricing per metric tonne

Sorting & Classification

Our experienced team manually and mechanically sorts all metal waste by type, grade, and purity — ensuring clean, contamination-free batches for downstream processing.

  • Magnetic separation of ferrous metals
  • Manual grading by experienced operators
  • Separation of aluminium, copper, brass, lead
  • Removal of non-metal contaminants
  • Grade certificates available on request

Baling & Processing

Post-sorting, metals are cleaned, cut to specification, and compressed into high-density bales ready for smelting, casting, or direct export.

  • Hydraulic baling to customer specification
  • Cut-to-size heavy scrap service
  • Turnings and borings processing
  • Wire-bound or loose bale options
  • Standard and custom bale dimensions

Logistics & Delivery

We handle the full logistics chain from our Anambra facility to your site — whether you are a local manufacturer or an international importer.

  • Road freight across Nigeria
  • Container stuffing for export
  • FOB Lagos port delivery
  • Ex-Works Anambra dispatch
  • Real-time shipment coordination

National & Global Distribution

Mechelin Metals supplies high-performance recycled raw materials to manufacturers both domestically and internationally — with a strong focus on West African and Asian markets.

  • Domestic supply to Nigerian manufacturers
  • Export to global buyers (Asia, Europe, Americas)
  • Volume contracts and long-term supply agreements
  • Port documentation support
  • LC and wire transfer payment flexibility

B2B Bulk Supply Contracts

For manufacturers requiring consistent, high-volume supply of recycled raw materials, we offer structured contract arrangements with guaranteed volumes, quality, and timelines.

  • Monthly/quarterly supply contracts
  • Dedicated account management
  • Priority processing and dispatch
  • Quality consistency guarantee
  • Flexible volume adjustments

Scrap Metal Consultation

Not sure what to do with your industrial or post-consumer metal waste? Our team can assess, classify, and recommend the optimal recycling or sale route for your scrap.

  • Free initial consultation
  • On-site scrap assessment
  • Grade and value estimation
  • Regulatory compliance guidance
  • Environmental disposal certificates

Custom Material Preparation

We prepare metals to exact buyer specifications — whether for foundry feed, smelter charge, or export grade — ensuring your material arrives ready to use.

  • Custom sizing and shredding
  • Alloy-specific sorting
  • Low residual specification batches
  • Export-ready containerisation
  • Material data sheets on request
