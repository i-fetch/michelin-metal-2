HOW WE DO IT

OUR PROCESSING FLOW

01

Sourcing

We collect metal waste from a wide network of suppliers including auto wreckers, demolition sites, and households.

02

Sorting

Metals are manually and magnetically separated by type, grade, and purity level.

03

Processing

Material is cleaned, cut to specification, compressed into bales or prepared in the required form.

04

Quality Check

Each batch is verified against grade specifications before packaging.

05

Packaging

Products are packaged appropriately — baled, drummed, or containerised per buyer requirements.

06

Distribution

Delivered nationally via road or internationally via containerised sea freight.
