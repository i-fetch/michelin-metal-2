ABOUT THE COMPANY

NIGERIA'S FOREMOST INTEGRATED METAL RECYCLER

Mechelin Metals Nigeria PVT LTD is a private company that leverages captive resources and advanced capabilities to source, sort and bale aluminium waste, distributing it as raw material to manufacturers. We handle all types of metals — from vehicles, household appliances, and building materials to all kinds of ferrous metals for sale, scrapping, conversion, or foundry purposes.

Formally incorporated in 2023, our team has been active in the industry for over a decade — building trust, infrastructure and global distribution networks that benefit both our clients and the environment.
